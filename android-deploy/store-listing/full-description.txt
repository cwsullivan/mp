Murder Poops - The Beet Reminder App

Have you ever eaten beets and then completely forgotten about it? Hours later, you visit the bathroom and experience a moment of sheer terror when you see red. Sound familiar?

Murder Poops is here to save you from unnecessary panic attacks!

HOW IT WORKS:
- Tap "I ate beets" after consuming beets
- Set your custom reminder time (default: 10 hours)
- When it's time, get a friendly reminder: "Don't panic! You're not dying. It's just beets."

FEATURES:
- Customizable reminder timing
- Multiple concurrent beet timers
- Cooldown period to prevent accidental double-taps
- Clean, fun interface
- No account required
- No data collection
- Works offline

Perfect for beet lovers, health enthusiasts, and anyone who enjoys a good vegetable but has a short memory!

Note: This app is for entertainment and personal reminder purposes only. If you have genuine health concerns, please consult a medical professional.
