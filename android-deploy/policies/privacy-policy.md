# Privacy Policy for Murder Poops

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

Murder Poops ("the App") is a simple beet reminder application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

Murder Poops does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data whatsoever.

## Local Storage

The App stores your settings and active reminders locally on your device only. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

This App does not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage
- Any external APIs

## Permissions

The App does not request any device permissions beyond basic app functionality.

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer.
